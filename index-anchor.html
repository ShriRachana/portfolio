transformers

forged to fight

Calling all Autobots, Decepticons, Maximals, and Predacons! Join Optimus Prime and the Earth Defense Command, who have crash-landed on a strange planet where multiple realities collide to form a massive planetary battlefield.

Assemble the ultimate team of Autobots and Decepticons from across time and space as you fight against a world of corrupted Transformers and their villainous overlords. Master intuitive touch controls in intense one-on-one combat, form mighty alliances and wage war against others, and create a fortified base to withstand the attacks of other players and house your riches.

With the Matrix of Leadership under your command, become the ultimate leader of the Transformers, and battle for supremacy in a planet full of danger, where friends become foes and foes becomes friends; in a world that is “More Than Meets the Eye.”

information

The primary goal of this Transformers: Forged To Fight Play-Together event is to obtain frequent feedback from you (via surveys) on how you feel about the game as you are playing it. Please be as honest and open as you can. Your input will have a big impact in making changes to the game prior to launch.


ON SURVEYS

Surveys will be short in length and there are opportunities for you to identify likes, dislikes, and improvements. As you are playing, we recommend that you keep a Word or Excel document with your detailed input for reference. You can then cut and paste it into the survey. Please include specifics on areas that you find frustrating or confusing.

You will get a series of three (3) surveys throughout the 14 day playtest. All surveys will be delivered via your work email. Please make sure you check your Spam folder if you don't receive them in your inbox.

Day 1 Survey will be delivered via email and should be completed at the end of your 1st day playing the game

Day 7 Survey will be delivered via email and should be completed at the end of the 7th day playing the game.

Day 14 Survey will be delivered via email and should be completed at the end of the 14 day play session

We ask that you play the game as you normally would. If you feel you want to stop playing early, please note where you are in the game, how you are feeling about it, and specifics on why you want to stop. Include all that information in the survey.

If you have any questions or you have not received your survey email, please reach out to Chris Langlois (clanglois@kabam.com)


ON CONTESTS & PRIZES

To thank you for your help and participation in this event, we are hosting a series of contests with a variety of prizes. Contests are held for both Gameplay achievements and for your feedback in the surveys. You will be entered into a Lucky Draw for each survey you complete and winners will be announced shortly after the deadline.

installation guides

** BEFORE YOU BEGIN, DELETE/UNINSTALL PRE-EXISTING “TRANSFORMERS:FORGED TO FIGHT” APP VERSIONS FROM YOUR DEVICE **

STEP 1. Install TestFlight on your iOS device

From your iOS device, launch the App Store and search for and install the “TestFlight” app

[Alternatively]

Paste the link -- https://itunes.apple.com /us/app/testflight/id899247664?mt=8 -- into your browser on your device

STEP 2. Install TRANSFORMERS: Forged to Fight (via Email & TestFlight)

[A] Open the TestFlight invitation email on your iOS device and select "Start Testing"

NOTE: If you are unable to locate the invitation email, check your Spam folder or contact Chinmay Banker (cbanker@kabaminc.com)

STEP 3. Click “Start Testing”

STEP 4. Click 'Redeem' and copy the redemption code (unique for each user)

STEP 5. Open the Testflight app on your device and click “Redeem”

NOTE: Your apple id (on your device) does NOT have to match the email id that you received the invite on.

STEP 6. Paste redemption code (that you copied in STEP E) and click “Redeem”

STEP 7. Once the installation is complete, click “Open” and begin playing!

** BEFORE YOU BEGIN, DELETE/UNINSTALL PRE-EXISTING "TRANSFORMERS: FORGED TO FIGHT" VERSIONS FROM YOUR DEVICE **

STEP 1. Open your testing group invitation on your Android device

Invite Link -- https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kabam.bigrobot --

By now, you should have received an email invitation notifying you that you have been added to the “bbot-beta-testers” google group

NOTE: If you are unable to locate the invitation email, check your Spam folder or contact Chinmay Banker (cbanker@kabaminc.com)

STEP 2. Click the 'Test' Link

Within the email, Click the testing link as shown below.

STEP 3. Become a Tester

On the next screen, click “Become a Tester”

STEP 4. Download TRANSFORMERS:Forged to Fight

Once the installation is complete, begin playing!
