transformers

forged to fight

Calling all Autobots, Decepticons, Maximals, and Predacons! Join Optimus Prime and the Earth Defense Command, who have crash-landed on a strange planet where multiple realities collide to form a massive planetary battlefield.

Assemble the ultimate team of Autobots and Decepticons from across time and space as you fight against a world of corrupted Transformers and their villainous overlords. Master intuitive touch controls in intense one-on-one combat, form mighty alliances and wage war against others, and create a fortified base to withstand the attacks of other players and house your riches.

With the Matrix of Leadership under your command, become the ultimate leader of the Transformers, and battle for supremacy in a planet full of danger, where friends become foes and foes becomes friends; in a world that is “More Than Meets the Eye.”

information

The surveys are designed to give frequent feedback on how you feel about the game as you are playing it. Please be as honest and open as you can. Your input will have a big impact in making changes to the game prior to launch.

The surveys will be short in length and there is an opportunity for you to identify likes, dislike, and improvements. As you are playing, we recommend that you keep a Word or Excel document with your detailed input for reference. You can then cut and paste it into the survey. Please include specifics on areas that you find frustration or confusing.

You will get a series of 3 surveys throughout the 14 day playtest. All surveys will be delivered via your work email. Please make sure you check your "spam" folder if you don't receive them in your inbox.

Day 1 Survey will be delivered via email and should be completed at the end of your 1st day playing the game Day 7 Survey will be delivered via email and should be completed at the end of the 7th day playing the game Day 14 Survey will be delivered via email and should be completed at the end of the 14 day play session

We ask that you play the game as you normally would. If you feel you want to stop playing early, please note where you are in the game, how you are feeling about it, and specifics on why you want to stop. Include all that information in the survey.

Please reach out to Chris Langlois if you have any questions or if you do not receive your survey email.

faq

How long is the play together? when am I expected to play?
The play together is two weeks long starting November 8th and ending November 18th. You can play any time that is convenient for you.
When will the build be available to download?
The build will be available November 8th, right after the kickoff email is received.
Which build do I install?
Android users, click here or the Android icon on the footer to begin installation. iOS users, download TestFlight from the e-mail that was sent and follow instructions given in the 'Installation Guide' section.
I need help downloading the build.
Please contact Chinmay Banker: cbanker@kabaminc.com.
I have an iOS device and I didn’t receive a TestFlight link.
Links will be emailed to your Kabam account on November 8th.Check your spam folder first, If you still don’t have it, please contact Chinmay Banker: cbanker@kabaminc.com. For more information on Installation read through the 'Installation Guide' section.
How many surveys do I have to complete?
That’s completely up to you :) However we encourage you to complete as many as possible. The information you provide is valuable and will help Transformers be Kabam’s next Smash!
I don’t play a lot of games. Is my feedback even useful?
Absolutely! Transformers is designed to engage casual players and hardcore gamers alike. We need your feedback, however minor. This is the whole reason for the company wide playtest!
I want to submit additional feedback
Great! Please submit feedback to bbot-playtogether-feedback@kabaminc.com.
How do I win prizes?
You must complete at least one Survey to be eligible for contest and prizes. For more information, refer the 'Contests/Prizes' section above.
I have a gameplay question such as “I’m stuck - how do I progress?”
Please email bbot-playtogether-help@kabaminc.com
How can I obtain hard currency? Can I purchase and be reimbursed?
Payments have been turned off since this build is running alongside the real world release.

installation guides

STEP A. Download Transformers

Click "Download" to save the transformers.apk file on your device.

NOTE: You must be connected to Kabam’s WiFi

STEP B: Install File Manager app

[1] Search for "File Manager" and install it from the Google Play store.

NOTE: You must be connected to Kabam’s WiFi

STEP C: Enable 3rd Party App Installation

[1] Go to Settings on your phone and switch to the “general” tab, if needed.

[2] Tap on the “Security” option.

[3] Tick the checkbox next to the “Unknown Sources” option.

[4] Confirm the warning message by tapping on “OK”.

STEP D: Install Game [via File Manager]

[1] Launch "File Manager" app [skip onscreen tutorial if prompted]

[2] Select "Download"

[3] Select “transformers.apk” installer file and launch it [downloaded from Step A].

[4] Select "Install" Transformers to install it on your device.

STEP E: Play Transformers

[1] Launch Transformers app

[2] Sign up using your Kabam email address: user@kabaminc.com

NOTE: DON'T FORGET TO HAVE FUN ! You must be connected to Kabam’s WiFi
